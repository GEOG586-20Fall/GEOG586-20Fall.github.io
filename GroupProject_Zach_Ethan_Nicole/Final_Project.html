Cryptids of North America

"Spooky Monsters"


This is a map of North American Cryptids.

This map will show you the location of cryptid sightings.

Click on the the popups to learn more about each cryptid and the sightings.




The cryptids are arranged by colour:

Brown = Bigfoot/Sasquatch

Turqoise = Ogopopgo

Hot Pink = Navajo Skinwalker

Yellow = Mothman

Red = Thunderbird

Navy Blue = Seelkee

Lime Green = Jersey Devil

Purple = Wendigo

Orange = Chupacbra
