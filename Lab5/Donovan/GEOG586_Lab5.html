Analyzing Wildfire Data in Alberta, 2000-2019

These maps show the 50 largest fires in 2 decades and the average maximum temperature, average minimum temperature, and total precipitation for that area.
The largest fires are indicated by the strongest colors and the color decreases as the size of the fire decreases.
The grey map shows the ecozone boundaries within the province which also contributes to the spatial distribution of wildfires. Click here to learn about the ecozones!
