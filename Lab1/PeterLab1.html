Peter's Calgary Key Attractions

By Yihuan Jiang

One of my favourite attraction is the Chinook Centre! It is the largest mall in Calgary and it has one of the city's biggest Cineplex theatre, which I adore and visit often!

Another personal favourite attraction is the Calgary Shooting Centre! It has a very nice indoor range for target practices and it has the largest collection of firearms in Calgary!

Lastly, I want to bring up a restaurant that I dore personally, which is the Five Guys Burgers and Fries! It is my go-to burger joint in Calgary!
