Brentwood Restaurants

As the new semester started, many students came back to the campus of the University of Calgary (UofC). Affected by COVID-19, the food court on campus that used to be popular among stuents could only provide limited food and services. In this circumstance, Brentwood shopping center, which is within walking distance of the University (around 10 minutes to walk from the Energy Environment and Experiential Learning Building) and accessible by public transits such as C-Train and bus, makes it a good alternative for students to buy food.
