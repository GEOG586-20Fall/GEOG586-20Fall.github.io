Hotspots for a student living in UofC Residence

A Guide by Mitchell Cornish, Residence Community Ambassador & Geographer

Welcome to my webpage! My name is Mitchell and I am a 3rd year Geography major, and this is also my third year living in residence at the University of Calgary. This semester, I am enrolled in GEOG 586 with Dr. Burns!
For this project, I wanted to map out the different hotspots for a student such as myself living in residence.
There are various areas I like to explore and visit during my days on and around campus. Take a look at the map to see some of my favourite places!

Living in residence has its perks, but it does suck to be on campus 24/7. My advice to students living here is to make sure you get off campus every once in a while!
Buses and LRT stations around campus are there to get you moving faster, and there are lots of things within walking distance.
