History!
  Supermarkets have long been associated with modernity in Iran.
  Etka Chain Stores Company, established in 1955, is controlled by 
  the economic holding company of Iran’s armed forces. 
The Tehran municipality launched the Shahrvand chain in 1993.  
Kourosh mall which has been established since 5 years ago, is located in western part of Tehran.
This outstanding mall has caused a traffic jam within the 1km redious, since it has great attraction for people.
Due to the concentration of a large number of facilities such as hospitals, university, parks, stadium and museum, 
this area is one of the most crowded areas of Tehran. Therefore, construction of Kourosh Mall in the center of 
this area has led to increase traffic congestion in it.
The town concils has provided some solutions in this Regard!
