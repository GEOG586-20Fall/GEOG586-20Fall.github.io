Peter's Calgary Key Attractions

By Yihuan Jiang

For those who love to do shopping, the Chinook Centre will be a must-visit location! It is the largest mall in Calgary and it has one of the city's biggest Cineplex theatre, which I adore and visit often!

For those who are interested in firearms, I would like to introduce the Calgary Shooting Centre! It has a very nice indoor range for target practices and it has the largest collection of firearms in Calgary!

For group entertainments, the Arcadia Adventures Escape Room is an optimal place! The Ye Olde Horrible Dungeon is my personal favourite with its Medieval atmosphere!

In terms of food, I want to bring up a restaurant that I dore personally, which is the Five Guys Burgers and Fries! It is my go-to burger joint in Calgary!

Lastly, for those who plan on doing some golfing, the Earl Grey Golf Club is a wonderful choice with its sub-urb location and the great views by the Glenmore Reservoir!
