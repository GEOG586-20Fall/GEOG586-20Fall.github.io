Solar Photovoltaic projects in Calgary, Alberta.

In 2012, “Funded by The City of Calgary’s Sustainable Buildings Partnership Program”, The City of Calgary has announced a plan to deploy solar power systems in Calgary in order to “reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 20 percent from 2005 to 2020 and reduced by 80 percent overall in 2050”. As a result, about 22 solar projects are currently active in Calgary and produces a total of more than 5500000000kWh annually (The City of Calgary, n.d).This web map is showing the projects' location, size and year of built.
