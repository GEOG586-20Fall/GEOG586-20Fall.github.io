Local Calgary Eateries

"For all the foodies out there"


One of the things I love most about Calgary is the great food it has to offer!

This map will show you some of my favourite places to eat in the inner city.

Click on a marker to explore the cities eateries.



The markers are color coded based on price range:

Pink = Low price range $

Purple = Medium price range $$

Red = High price range $$$


The blue polygon overlays 17th Avenue. 17th is home to some of the best food in Calgary, A trip to 17th is a guarentee for some good food.If none of the eateries on this map suit you I recommend checking out more of 17th Ave
