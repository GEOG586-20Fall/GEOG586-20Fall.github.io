About Pennsylvania

Welcome to Pennsylvania! Pennsylvania is one of fifty US states, and has a population of 12.8 million people as of 2019.
The capital city of Pennsylvania is Harrisburg, represented by the blue pin on the map.
. Travelling to Pennsylvania soon? All of our counties have amazing tourism activities available. While travelling in Pennsylvania, please remember to follow all COVID-19 procedures, and if you are coming from out of country, please self-isolate for 14 days upon arrival.
